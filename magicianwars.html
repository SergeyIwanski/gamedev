A game like Worms, but instead of worms, magicians and knights

Different types of units have different ability sets

At the beginning of the round, units, buildings and defensive installations are purchased

If a player loses all of his units, he defeated

The game ends when 1 player is left

4 people can play

 

Graphics - 3D, as in children's cartoons

Bright colors, soft outlines

 

Voice and animations should be in a cartoonish, playful way

 

Special effects, such as fire, lightning, must be realistic, and all sounds, too.
