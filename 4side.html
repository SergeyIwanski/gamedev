Board game in the genre of monopoly.

 

This is the same World

At the same time.

Different sides of one reality.

You can move between them!

But not everyone knows about it,

And they should not know about it...

 

Purpose of the game: to capture all the buildings or stay alive.

 

At the beginning of the game, 1000 krediks are given (kr) and the player at the beginning of the game can buy items on them: an adapter, a destructor, ...

 

You can buy a building, activate an artifact after a move has been made (and after an event has occurred).

 

You can buy an artifact on a special cage, or in the general game store for premium currency.

 

There are conditions when your buildings are inactive. You cannot sell them.

During this time, players do not pay on your buildings. In addition, they can outbid them.

In this case, money is given into the game, and the building card is taken from the current owner.

 

Slavery: You are given hard work, and every day (move) you must bring 2kr to the owner, otherwise you will be killed!

Each turn you are given 0-5kr (i.e. during work you found something). There are 4 ways to get out of slavery:

1: Give the owner 500kr for freedom.

2: to escape, but this opportunity will appear if another player on the same map passes by (then you move to the same cell as the other player, and the event of the cell that you switched to is activated)

3: use adapter

4: use destructor

Note: During slavery, your buildings are inactive and may be taken (bought) by other players.

 

Sides (parallel realities):
1. Nature, warmth, greenery, all peaceful and kind,

but at the same time poor

 

2. High technology, career growth, law and order, social protection

eternal winter, cold, bureaucracy, fraud, fictitious titles (leader, administrator, minister...)

 

3. Freedom, money,

desert, looters, post-apocalypse

 

4. Industry, cheap buildings,

volcano eruption, devastation, slavery, crime

 

Items:

Adapter - for the transition between dimensions, allows you to move the player if he is on the same cell and in the same reality.

Destructor - destroys the building and removes all the money of all players on the same cell in all dimensions at the same time prevents the cell event.

Synchronizer - allows you to copy money and items of the player in any reality (completely replaces your own), for this you need to get on the cell with the player

Destabilizer - activates the specified item of the player and allows you to use it on behalf of this player, for this you need to be on the same cell and in the same reality with the player.

Protector - neutralizes the actions of other players against you, triggered automatically.
