The Last Prophecy - RTS

[Camp / shelter affected by the experiment]

 

When they entered the tent, a dying old man lay in the twilight. Near him stood other people.

When the soldier approached him, the old man said in a hoarse voice:

 

" Judgment Day is сoming.

Liberator, Defender, Guard and Peacemaker

They will pass on earth, replacing each other

Carrying chaos and destruction

The Liberator will announce about Judgment Day

And the Guard will rise up against its creators

And Defender will unite with the Liberator to overthrow the Guard

But they will disappear together,

The Peacemaker will come instead,

Bringing with him plague and cataclysms.

Rain of fire will fall from heaven

And the dead will rise

And they will be together with the living in the Great War.

A bright flash will appear in the sky, and put an end to the war.

After this, a new era will come.

Our era will end... "

 

 

Between different states, military operations are taking place. Science corporations supply their weapons and defense. World on the verge of World War.

 

The extremist group calling themselves Liberation decided to "clear the world of corrupt authorities and greedy corporations." They make sabotage, diversions, provoke riots.

Operation "Judgment Day": A wave of terrakts swept the Earth, sweeping away governments and science firms one after another. Vast territory under the control of the Liberation.

They are opposed by an alliance of powerful organizations that have fantastic equipment, the latest technology. Despite the fact that their actions are aimed at defense people and the environment, they cause no less harm.

 

Liberation developed a computer virus that changes the behavior of AI and thereby incapacitates all equipment. They make their way to the communication center and launch the malicious program. But something went wrong. "Smart" robots get out of control and begin to attack their own. Machines take over the city and it spreads further. Corporations are forced to abandon many of their AI-based technologies, come up with different ways to neutralize the virus, but it doesn’t work out very well for them, since AI is constantly improving itself. Unusual constructions and cyborgs with new properties appear. All of them are under the control of the defensive system "Guard".

The Alliance and Liberation unite and jointly defeat the cyborgs, but they themselves suffer huge losses. Corporations taking advantage of this take power into their own hands and promise to build a world without wars.

 

It became known that near the Earth a cluster of asteroids will fly by and some of them will collapse on Earth. Perhaps this was due to some unsuccessful experiment.

From time to time, in some territories, meteorites and fragments of extraterrestrial ships fall.

All parties suffer destruction. But corporations were able to find the benefit. In the wreckage, they find new minerals, energy sources, explore new technologies. A large-scale confrontation between corporations and an arms race is unfolding.

 

People began to notice how those who had previously died in the war now act on the battlefield. Some of them have mechanical parts of the body. Eyewitnesses saw how cyborgs dragged away the killed people.

 

The corporate war is expanding at full strength.
